Important FAQ

What is Donate Bangladesh?

Answer: Donate Bangladesh is a platform dedicated to raising funds to support people affected by the recent floods in Bangladesh. Our mission is to provide relief and essential supplies to those in need.

How will my donation be used?

Answer: Your donation will go directly towards providing food, clean water, medical supplies, shelter, and other necessary items to the flood-affected communities in Bangladesh.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Answer: Yes, all donations made through Donate Bangladesh are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt via email for your records.

Can I donate items instead of money?

Answer: Currently, we are only accepting monetary donations, as this allows us to quickly and efficiently purchase supplies in bulk to meet the urgent needs of flood victims.

How can I ensure my donation is being used responsibly?

Answer: We are committed to transparency and accountability. Regular updates on how the funds are being utilized, along with photos and reports from the ground, will be shared on our website and social media platforms.
